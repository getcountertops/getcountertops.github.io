With 30 years of experience, we're ready to turn your vision into a lasting reality.

Central Florida Marble is a family owned and operated business that has been serving Central Florida since 1987. Our company has always prided itself on quality work and outstanding customer service. We specialize in the fabrication and installation of countertops, vanity tops, and several other household surfaces, providing our customers with high quality product options in natural granite, marble and quartzite as well as the top brands of man-made quartz. We have been fabricating Dupont Corian for over 20 years and also work with other leading brands of Solid Surface. Our company is unique in that we are also a manufacturer of custom cultured marble. We offer a wide array of products to accommodate the customer's preferences and budget and work side by side with them to meet their needs. We have several years of experience fabricating for the two largest box stores as well as many custom builders and millwork companies, always earning excellent ratings.

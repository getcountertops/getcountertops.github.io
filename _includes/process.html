With You Each Step

Moving from your vision to finished countertops is as easy as Four Steps.

Step 1: Measurements, Material Choice, and Price Estimate

Whether you something or are just getting started, we'll guide you to the perfect surface each step of the way.

Describe Your Project

To get started, give us a call and describe your project. It is helpful if you can give the measurements both of the countertop area and built-in appliances and faucets that will be installed.

Visit Our Showroom

Not sure which material is right for you? Make an appointment to visit our showroom. We will help you select a product, color, and edge profile that fits your needs and budget. Once you've made your decision, we'll give you a price estimate based on the size of the project and the material used. Remnant pieces are available for use in smaller projects.

Step 2:Finalized Measurements and Price

Next, our team will visit the job site and take final measurements of the countertop area. After creating the template, we will send the finalized price.

Step 3: Fabrication

We fabricate all our household surfaces using the latest industry technology, including CNC Machinery to shape our quartz, quartzite and granite products.

Step 4: Installation

Time between choosing the material and the final installation will vary based on the size of the project.

A Legacy of Quality Work

My name is Susan Gustafson, President of Central Florida Marble. Our family-owned and operated company has served Central Florida and surrounding areas since 1987, providing countertops and other surfaces to leading custom builders, millwork companies, and the top two home improvement companies in the United States.

Imagine the Possabilities

We specialize in the fabrication and installation of countertops and other household surfaces, providing our customers with high quality product options in natural granite, marble and quartzite, as well as the top brands of man-made quartz and solid surface.

We are a certified fabricator of Cambria, Wilson Art, and Dupont Zodiaq and Corian surfaces. We have fabricated Dupont Corian for over 20 years.

Cultured Marble Specialty

Our company is unique because we also manufacture custom cultured marble. The material is similar to traditional solid surfaces but resembles natural marble stone and comes with a high-gloss finish.

We fabricate cultured marble vanity tops, which can come with a integral backsplash and molded sink, as well as cultured marble thresholds, window sills, and fireplace, showers and tub deck surrounds.

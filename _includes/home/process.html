A Straightfoward Process

Crafting the cornerstone of your kitchen is as easy as Four Simple Steps.

Step 1: Measurements, Material Choice, and Price Estimate

To get started, give us a call and describe your project. It is helpful if you can give the measurements both of the countertop area and built-in appliances and faucets that will be installed.

Visit Our Showroom

Not sure which material is right for you? Make an appointment to visit our showroom. We will help you select a product, color, finish and edge profile that fits your needs and budget. We have a selection of stone remnants available for use in smaller projects.

Get Your Estimate

Once you've made your decision, we'll give you a price estimate based on the size of the project and the material used.

Step 2: Finalized Measurements and Price

Next, our team will visit the job site and take final measurements of the countertop area. After creating the template, we will send the finalized price.

Step 3: Fabrication

We fabricate all our household surfaces using the latest industry technology, including CNC Machinery to shape our quartz, quartzite and granite products.

Step 4: Installation

Time between choosing the material and the final installation will vary based on the size of the project.

Call Us Today

Ready to get started? Whether you have measurements in hand or just want to learn more, give us a call and we will be glad to answer your questions and guide you to the next step. Schedule an appointment now to visit our showroom and view swatches in person.
